About College Level Counselling Portal

Transforming the college admission process with transparency and efficiency

Our Mission

The College Level Counselling (CLC) Portal is designed to ensure a fair and transparent admission process for students based on their exam ranks. Our platform streamlines the counselling process, making it easier for students to secure admission without manual intervention or bias.

We believe every student deserves an equal opportunity to pursue education at the institution of their choice based on merit. By digitizing and automating the counselling process, we eliminate potential bias and ensure that admissions are allocated strictly according to rank and availability.

Key Features

Rank-Based Admission

Our system ensures that admissions are strictly based on merit, considering students' ranks in qualifying exams.

Real-time Rankings

Students can check their current standing in real-time, increasing transparency in the admission process.

Seat Matrix

Comprehensive view of available seats across different branches, updated in real-time as admissions progress.

Secure Process

Multi-level authentication and role-based access ensure the integrity and security of the admission process.

User Roles

Student

  • Register on the system
  • Login to the portal
  • View current position
  • Track admission status

Admission Officer

  • Admit students
  • Process student applications
  • Update vacant seats
  • Manage student details

Admin

  • Add/remove admission officers
  • Oversee complete system
  • Manage user permissions
  • System administration

How It Works

1

Registration

Students register using their qualifying exam roll number, providing their personal details, rank, and other required information.

2

Seat Allocation

Admission officers update the vacant seat matrix at the beginning of the counselling process for each branch.

3

Counselling

Students are called one by one based on their rank. Those with valid documents can select from available seats.

4

Confirmation

Once a student is admitted, the seat is automatically deducted from the vacant seats matrix and updated in real-time.
